Welcome back to Gentle Readers! Enjoy exploring new books and connecting with fellow readers.

If you haven't already, be sure to check out your Book Club! Head to the Book Club Home at the top to:

  • Vote on Books: Help your club decide what to read next.
  • Join Discussions: Share your thoughts and engage with other readers.
  • Check Your Calendar: Stay updated with upcoming meetings and events.
  • Select Books: Start reading and explore new books to add to your collection.

Whether you're an active participant or just getting started, there's always something exciting happening in your book club!

If you're curious about the most popular books, head over to the Search Books tab at the top to explore the bestsellers and trending reads!

Or, if you'd rather start your own book club, click on Create Club at the top to get started!
